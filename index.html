safeID is a communication solution for those who wish to compile and efficiently display their medical and allergy information.

Not only does safeID allow for the automatic creation of formatted ID card displays but also allows the integrated capability to translate this information into any of the supported languages.

Exploring safeID

Customizable Information

Our information entry page has many of the most common allergies and medical conditions, with more being added as the app undergoes further development. Information is separated into various categories pertaining to the items and the display cards.

Efficiency Across Language Barriers

Our application prioritizes efficient communication above all else, even across language barriers. The ID is not only clear and compact but also, when created through our edit page, is capable of being translated into any of the supported languages available in the home menu.

Communicate Lots of Information in Little Time

safeID has both a full featured medical ID, as well as a limited “restaurant ID” for the display of information like allergies without displaying more personal medical conditions or information
